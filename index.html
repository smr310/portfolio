About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer based in Boston, and I build clean, responsive, user-friendly web applications. I frequently use React/Redux and Node.js/Express, and I am always eager to learn new technologies and skills. I welcome challenges with open arms and thrive when pushed outside of my comfort zone and in fast-paced environments.

When I am not coding, I can usually be found in the garage wrenching on my car or motorcycle, enjoying a barbeque with family and friends, or on a travel adventure with my wife, by motorcycle when she approves!

My Projects

TravelBak

TravelBak is a full stack journal application for keeping track of your travel adventures. Users can create new trips and then add / edit / delete journal entries for each trip.

Motorsport Track Days

Motorsport Track Days is a full stack application built for motorsport event companies that organize race track days. Attendees can register for events and specify their rental equipment requirements.

Good Scenes

Good Scenes communicates with two third-party APIs to randomly generate a famous movie scene with the click of a button.
